# Course Structure (6 Weeks)

## Week 1: Foundations
- [Lesson 1](../lessons/lesson1.qmd): Cartesian coordinates and plotting
- [Lesson 2](../lessons/lesson2.qmd): Maps, scale, bearings
- [Lesson 3](../lessons/lesson3.qmd): Distance formula
- Application and short practice tasks

## Week 2: Midpoint and Gradients
- [Lesson 4](../lessons/lesson4.qmd): Midpoint formula
- [Lesson 5](../lessons/lesson5.qmd): Gradient basics (rise/run)
- [Lesson 6](../lessons/lesson6.qmd): Gradient using the formula
- Practice and real-world gradient interpretation

## Week 3: Lines and Relationships
- [Lesson 7](../lessons/lesson7.qmd): Linear equations and intersections
- [Lesson 8](../lessons/lesson8.qmd): Parallel lines
- [Lesson 9](../lessons/lesson9.qmd): Perpendicular lines
- Application with mixed line problems

## Week 4: Consolidation of Line Equations
- [Lesson 10](../lessons/lesson10.qmd): Equation from gradient and a point
- Practice with intersections, parallel and perpendicular lines
- Mixed-problem sessions to connect methods

## Week 5: Practice Focus
- [Lesson 11](../lessons/lesson11.qmd): Practice and revision
- Targeted practice blocks and NZQA-style questions

## Week 6: Practice and Assessment
- **Three practice lessons** using [Lesson 11](../lessons/lesson11.qmd)
- **Two assessment lessons** (see [Assessment](../assessment/index.qmd))
