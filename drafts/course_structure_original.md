# Course Structure (Original)

## Week 1: Fundamentals
- Introduction to the Cartesian coordinate system
- Plotting points and basic concepts
- Distance formula and applications
- Midpoint formula and practice

**Homework**: Complete Worksheet 1 (Coordinate Basics)

## Week 2: Lines and Gradients
- Understanding gradient (slope)
- Equations of lines in various forms
- Parallel and perpendicular lines
- Problem-solving with linear equations

**Homework**: Complete Worksheet 2 (Gradient and Equations)

## Week 3: Problem Solving and Assessment Preparation
- Real-world applications of coordinate geometry
- Complex problem-solving strategies
- Practice assessment tasks (Achievement, Merit, Excellence levels)
- Review and consolidation
- Final assessment

**Homework**: Complete Worksheet 3 and practice assessment tasks
