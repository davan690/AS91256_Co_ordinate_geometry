Study Tips & Strategies

Recommended Study Approach

  1. Master the basics first - Ensure you can plot points and calculate distances
  2. Practice regularly - 15-20 minutes daily is better than cramming
  3. Check your work - Use multiple methods to verify answers
  4. Draw diagrams - Visualize every problem
  5. Learn from mistakes - Review incorrect answers carefully

Common Pitfalls to Avoid

  • Mixing up x and y coordinates
  • Forgetting the square root in distance formula
  • Sign errors when calculating gradient
  • Not simplifying final answers
  • Rushing through multi-step problems
